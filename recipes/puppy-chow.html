Puppy Chow

A dessert by any other name would taste as delicious.

This delicious dessert that combines cereal with choclate, peanut butter,

and powdered sugar, is known by many different names. Regardless of what

you call it though, it's always the perfect snack for any occasion.

  • 1 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 6 cups of rice chex cereal
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  1. Melt the peanut butter and chocolate together.
  2. Add cereal to the peanut butter/chocolate mix and stir until evenly coated.
  3. Cool the mixture in the fridge to room temperature.
  4. Add powdered sugar and mix until combined.(Feel free to add more sugar if that's your thing.)
  5. Dump everything into a bowl and set out for friends and family to enjoy.
