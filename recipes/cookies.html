Cutout Cookies

Sugar Cookies with some Personality

These delicious sugar cookies are meant to be cutout and decorated with frosting

before being devoured or left out for Santa Claus.

  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 4 tbsp of milk
  1. Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla together.
  2. Add in egg and beat.
  3. Add remaining dry ingredients alternativley with milk.
  4. Knead until even consistency.
  5. Wrapped in wax paper and chill in refrigerator overnight.
  6. Roll out dough on table and, using cookie cutters, cut out as many shapes as you want.
  7. Transfer cutout cookies to baking tray and bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
  8. Once cookies are cooled, decorate with frosting.
